Police Chicken!

We are currently working on a computer animated 2d movie. The Police Chicken Movie. It does include stop motion animation but the film is mostly 2d animation. This movie that we are currently working on, is still in the very early stages of planning, story boarding, finding voice actors, animators and other people that will work on the film. Police Chicken is a… well… a Police Chicken. He is a one of a kind creature fighting for good. And in this case fighting against The Big Bad Bunny!

Police Chicken Is a film that Ambrose Animation are currently working on. In it, there is the main character, Police chicken, and his enemy, The Big Bad Bunny. Police Chicken is as close to being a superhero without being one, as you can get. Thought at the start of the movie police Chicken is applying to be a superhero. The Police Chicken Movie is a comedy and an adventure movie with laughs and scares. But police chicken isn't just a movie. Police chicken is a character with a movie based on him.
