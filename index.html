Hi. I'm Ambrose Animation. I make stop motion animation videos and try to upload them every other Saturday or Sunday! Check them out my channel here: Ambrose Animtion.

Here at Ambrose animation we aspire to create happiness throughout others. We create good quality Lego and plasticine stop motion or occasionally computer animations. We try to bring joy to people’s day by making them smile grin or even through back their heads and laugh!

We try to inspire others to create animations, as in your house you are likely to have good materials for making animations with. You don’t need a massive set and figurines made with expensive armatures. You could simply pick up a Lego minifigure and a simple set with a handful of bricks. Or you could make a plasticine figure and animate its hand moving against a sheet of card. Or combine a plasticine person Infront of a Lego set. Of a Lego man Infront of a sheet of card! The possibilities are endless! Just make your having fun doing what you’re doing!
